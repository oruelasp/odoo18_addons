Database Create Internal Requisition

Employees easily select required products, specify quantities, and add detailed requirements through an intuitive interface.

Submit for Approval

One-click submission sends the requisition to designated approvers. Status automatically updates to Submitted with email notifications sent to all stakeholders.

Review & Approve

Approvers receive instant notifications and can review, approve, or reject requests with detailed comments. Status updates to Approved upon approval.

Automatic Purchase Integration

Approved requisitions seamlessly integrate with the Purchase module as Blanket Orders, ready for conversion to RFQs and purchase orders.
